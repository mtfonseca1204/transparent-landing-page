Markets Made Visible

We provide real-time visibility into price-peg health, liquidity depth, and execution quality across target pools. We alert you when pools depeg or incentives drift, diagnose the underlying bottlenecks, and translate those signals into actionable insights for SLAs and incentive design.

Under The Surface

Pools are more than LP positions. We surface the signals that actually determine whether your users get good prices, and what to do when they don't.

Real-Time Alerts

Instant notifications when pools depeg, depth deteriorates, or execution quality drops. Surface problems the moment they start—not after your users have already felt them.

Pegging & Price Quality

Continuous monitoring of pegging health and price accuracy across pools. When a pool is performing well, trace exactly what's driving it—so you can replicate the conditions elsewhere.

Structural Diagnostics

Not every pool has the same rebalancing dynamics. Map the underlying constraints—withdrawal paths, bridge availability, arbitrage accessibility—that shape pegging efficiency per asset.

Incentive Effectiveness

See whether incentive spend is translating into better markets or just getting absorbed. Connect SLA performance to actual pricing outcomes—so every dollar deployed improves the experience your users feel.

Beyond The MM

Healthy pools aren't always about the primary LP. Organic arbitrage, ecosystem activity, and asset accessibility all play a role. See the full picture of what's actually keeping your markets tight.

Operational Clarity

Move from gut feel to evidence. SLA reviews, incentive reallocation, and market maker conversations grounded in data—focused on outcomes, not finger-pointing.
